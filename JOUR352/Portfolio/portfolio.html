About Me


My name is Adelia McGuire and I am an undergraduate journalism major and business minor from Madison, Connecticut. I am currently enrolled at the University of Maryland where I am admitted to the Philip Merrill College of Journalism and the Robert H. Smith School of Business. I am passionately pursuing a track for multimedia journalism.

Email: adeliamcguire@gmail.com
Phone Number: +1 (203)-988-2214

I am currently a staff writer for The Campus Trainer, Monumental Magazine, and Her Campus. I actively take freelance writing opportunities to enhance my writing craft. I have recently been published in the Hyattsville Life and Times as well as the Prince George's County Sentinel. Last Spring I joined the Maryland Baseball Network where I covered games and worked as a beat writer. This year I was accepted onto the Big Ten Plus student committee. I run the scorebox and xpressions for women's volleyball which allows me to interact with other student journalists while learning about what goes into producing a live broadcast. In the past two years of my education at UMD I have explored a passion for social media and marketing, in my first two semesters I began working on the social media and engagement committees of two on-campus publications. Now, as a sophomore, I have been selected for a position on the executive board for both of these publications and have been promoted as the editor of social media. I am responsible for the complete running of all social platforms and oversee a team of social media committee members. Beyond journalism, I am actively engaged in my community and on campus. I am a member of the Alpha Phi sorority where I engage with other Greek life organizations in volunteer and fundraising opportunities. Through Alpha Phi, I joined the Ten Terp Plan for Sexual Assault, a prevention program that aims to create a positive cultural change within chapters and across the broader Sorority and Fraternity community.
